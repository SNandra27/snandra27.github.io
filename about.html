Contact Me

A Little Bit of History

I have come from a technical background where I spent my days coding using ASP.Net C#. After around 10 years of this I felt it was time for a change, and decided to take a side step into the world of Quality Assurance. I found Quality Assurance very enjoyable especially the psychology behind why someone would do a certain thing when using a site/app and how depending on who it is, the result can change.

Without consciously realising, I discovered UX Design and immediately fell in love. In order to gain more knowledge I have spent the past year studying many online courses and doing small projects to bring that knowledge to life. The studying won't end anytime soon, as I believe that is the key to keeping the passion alive and solving the problems in our constantly changing world.

My Mission

As UX Design continues to fascinate me, I would like to really start making a positive impact on people's lives and help them fall in love with the websites and mobile apps they use, whether that's once in a while or everyday. A good experience means they will keep on coming back. How do I know this? Because I am one of them. By making this personal, I can empathise with my users and put myself in their shoes.

More About Me

When I am not trying to save the world one UX design at a time, I love to lose myself in a good book, play music very loud, or get outside and hike amongst the nature. Socialising with my friends and family is also very important to me, and it usually involves a large amount of food and laughter.

What's Next

Right now I would like to be part of a team who share my passion for UX Design, where we can bounce ideas of each other and together keep improving each other and the work that we do. I would like to be able to make contributions to the company that I work for, so that the difference is felt by the users in many years to come.
